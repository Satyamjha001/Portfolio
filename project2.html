PROJECT 2

Project Overview

Title of the project:
AI integrated with IOT based approach for early fish disease detection.

Description of the Project:
This project aims to leverage IoT technology and artificial intelligence (AI) to enable early detection of diseases in fish farms. By integrating sensors with IoT devices and implementing AI algorithms, the system can monitor water quality,water level,Oxygen level and PH level parameters all these value store in cloud storage(thingspeak) and detect abnormalities indicative of potential fish diseases. Additionally, the system utilizes AI to count the number of fish in the farm, providing valuable insights to the fish farmers.
